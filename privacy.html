Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us.

At YUSTAM Marketplace, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information for everyone in our marketplace community.

Information We Collect

  • Account details shared by vendors for onboarding, verification, and payout setup.
  • Listing information including product descriptions, pricing, and uploaded media files.
  • Support communications and chat history that help us respond to enquiries.
  • Analytics and usage data that guide platform improvements and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate marketplace features, publish listings, and facilitate vendor success.
  • To keep vendors and shoppers informed through email, SMS, or in-app notifications.
  • To uphold community safety, detect suspicious activity, and prevent fraud.
  • To analyse performance metrics and deliver a smoother shopping experience.

Sharing & Disclosure

YUSTAM does not sell or rent personal data. Information is shared only with trusted service providers that support marketplace operations, such as payment processors, hosting partners, or compliance tools.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to measure engagement, personalise content, and continuously improve the marketplace. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, although some features may be limited.

Data Security

Your information is securely stored in Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest. Routine security reviews and audits help us meet Nigerian data protection requirements and industry best practices.

Your Rights

You can request access, corrections, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Vendors also have the option to deactivate their accounts directly from their dashboards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect new features or regulatory requirements. We’ll publish changes on this page, and continuing to use YUSTAM signifies acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

Have questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@yustam.com or visit our Contact page for more ways to reach the team.
